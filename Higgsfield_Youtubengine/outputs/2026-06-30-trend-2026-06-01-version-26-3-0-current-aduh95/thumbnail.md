# Thumbnail Concept — 2026-06-01, Version 26.3.0 (Current), @aduh95

- **Primary image:** the most striking frame from Scene 4 (the TURN/PAYOFF beat).
- **Composition:** subject large, off-center; eyes/face or key object in the top two-thirds.
- **Text:** 2–4 huge words, high contrast (e.g. "2026-06-01, VERSION 26.3").
- **Color:** lead with the channel's ownable accent color; dark vignette for pop.
- **Emotion:** one clear emotion on the subject (curiosity, shock, awe).
- **Readability:** must read at phone-thumbnail size; test at 120px wide.
- **Consistency:** same font + placement every video to build channel recognition.
