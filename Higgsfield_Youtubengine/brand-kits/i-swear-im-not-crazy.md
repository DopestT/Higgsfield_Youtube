# Brand Kit — I Swear I'm Not Crazy

Channel: youtube.com/@swearimnotcrazy. Trending news/tech/culture commentary —
the pattern underneath the headline, before it's obvious to everyone else.

## Identity
- **Channel name:** I Swear I'm Not Crazy
- **Handle:** @swearimnotcrazy
- **Tagline:** ...and neither are you.
- **One-line promise:** What's actually happening in the story everyone's covering wrong — explained before it's consensus.
- **Accent color (ownable):** #TBD — pick one bold, high-contrast color (suggest a warning-adjacent amber/red or an electric teal; avoid generic tech blue/purple)
- **Secondary palette:** near-black background, off-white text

## Visual language
- **Style:** cinematic, high-contrast, shallow depth of field, filmic grade, documentary realism
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16
- **Signature motif:** a recurring "connect the dots" visual — two seemingly unrelated things pulled into frame and linked (mirrors the "nobody's connecting these" voice)
- **Caption font + placement:** bold sans-serif, lower-third, all-caps, high contrast against the accent color

## Voice
- **VO voice:** confident, dry, slightly conspiratorial but grounded — not shouty, not panicked
- **Tone words:** vindicated, sharp, contrarian-but-right, "called it," grounded (never unhinged)

## Script voice rules (used by the production engine)
- **Hook:** open with a claim that sounds like a stretch, then immediately back it with the signal.
  Examples: "I called this two weeks ago." / "Nobody's connecting these two stories. I am." /
  "This is going to sound insane until you see the pattern."
- **Never use generic clickbait** ("Stop scrolling", "You won't believe"). Replace with
  vindication-forward framing — the viewer already suspected this; we're confirming it.
- **Button/close:** end on a confidence beat, not a soft CTA.
  "You don't have to believe me yet. Just remember I said it." + follow prompt.
- **Hashtag/CTA tone:** dry confidence, not hype. Avoid "#fyp"-style desperation; prefer
  "#calledit" / "#patternrecognition" / topic-specific tags.

## Recurring character (optional)
- None yet — persona-driven (voice/host energy), not a mascot.

## End-card / signature stamp
- Final frame: the "connect the dots" motif fully linked, with the tagline
  "...and neither are you." as the closing text overlay.
